The Premise: You are a rookie adventurer trapped in a 3-floor mini-dungeon. To escape, you must defeat the enemies on each floor, find the key to the stairs, and eventually defeat the "Slime King" on the final floor.

Core Mechanics:

Movement: Grid-based or simple free-movement top-down mechanics (which you've already experimented with!).

Combat: Simple bumper combat (walking into an enemy attacks them) or a basic spacebar swing.

Progression: Defeating enemies drops gold or small potions that restore health.
